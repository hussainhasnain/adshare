Privacy Notice

Updated Date: 02 August 2023

1.  Introduction

Thank you for choosing AdShare Limited. We are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Notice outlines our practises regarding the collection, use, disclosure, and protection of your information when you interact with our website or use our services. By accessing or using our website and services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Notice.

2.  About us

AdShare Limited is a leading provider of email and digital marketing services to business, non-profits and organisations, these services provide relevant marketing communications across different platforms. Personal information and other data provided by our clients contact lists (this may include email address and other information) and content (including the contact of their campaigns), this information is controlled by our clients.

3.  Information we collect

a.

Personal Information: We may collect various types of personal information that you provide to us voluntarily, such as your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and other contact details. This information is typically collected when you sign up for an account, subscribe to our newsletters, participate in surveys, or contact us for customer support.

b.

Usage Information: When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your interactions with our site. This may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, device information, referring URL, pages viewed, and the dates and times of your visits. We use this information to analyse user behaviour, improve website functionality, and better understand how visitors use our services.

c.

Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and gather information about your activities on our website. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us recognise you and remember your preferences. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

4.  How we use your information

a.

Providing and improving services: We use your personal information to provide the services you requested, such as processing orders, delivering products or services, and sending transactional communications. We may also use your information to improve our services, tailor content to your interests, and enhance the overall user experience.

b.

Communication: We may use your contact information to communicate with you about your account, respond to inquiries, and provide customer support. With your consent, we may also send you promotional emails, newsletters, and updates about our products, services, and special offers. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by following the instructions in the emails or contacting us directly.

c.

Analytics: We analyse usage patterns and website traffic to gain insights into user behaviour, evaluate the effectiveness of marketing campaigns, and make data-driven decisions to enhance our offerings. This helps us understand user preferences and tailor our content and services accordingly.

5.  Sharing your information

a.

Service Providers: We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services, such as payment processors, shipping companies, and marketing partners. They are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not allowed to use your information for any other purpose.

b.

Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law, in response to a valid legal request, or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property. This includes sharing information with law enforcement agencies, regulatory authorities, or other third parties as necessary.

c.

Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganisation, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your personal information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you via email or prominent notice on our website if such a transfer occurs and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

6.  Your choices and rights

a.

Opt-Out: If you no longer wish to receive marketing communications from us, by contacting us using the information provided in the "Get Help" section below.

b.

Access and Update: You have the right to access, correct, or update your personal information. If you would like to review or modify the information we have about you, please contact us using the details provided in the "Get Help" section below.

c.

Data Retention: We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Privacy Notice or as required by law. Once your data is no longer needed, we will securely dispose of it or anonymize it for analytical purposes..

7.  Data security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
